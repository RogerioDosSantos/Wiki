Limited Liability Company (LLC)

Opening an LLC

Start the Business

The incfile.com site allows you to start the business only paying the State Fee. On the state of Texas, the overall process took 03 days.

Apply to Employer Identification Number (EIN)

You can apply to the EIN online trough the IRS (EIN).

You will need:

  • Your Social Security
  • LLC Address
  • LLC Phone
  • LLC Legal Name
  • LLC Start Date
  • LLC Principal Business Activity

Open a Bank Account

Bank of America allows you to create a business account that you can avoid fees if you have $3000.00 in the account. It also have a cache back business credit card account without anual fee.

To open the account you will need:

  • LLC Information
  • LLC EIN

Transfer a Real State Property to the LLC

If your property is in a mortgage contract, be sure that you get a writen consent from you morgage company agreeing with the transfer before you execute it. Otherwise the mortgage company can consider your contract null and you will have to pay the remaining debt in full.

If you are the sole owner, you can do it by creating a Quit Claim Deed Form.

You will need:

  • LLC Information
  • Detail Property Information

Once the Quit Claim Deed Form is filled you need to sign it in front of a notary. Bank of America offers to its customers a Notary Service. All you need to do is to take the document to an agency with a photo document that proves your identity.
